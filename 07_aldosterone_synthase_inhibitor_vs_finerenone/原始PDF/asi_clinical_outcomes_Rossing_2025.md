## Study design (NCT05182840, phase 2)

- Multinational, randomized, dose-finding phase 2 trial of the highly selective aldosterone synthase inhibitor **vicadrostat (BI 690517)**, alone or in combination with the SGLT2 inhibitor **empagliflozin (EMPA 10 mg)**, in adults with CKD with or without type 2 diabetes (T2D), all on maximally tolerated renin-angiotensin system inhibition. *(Source: Crossref/abstract; AJKD companion paper via DocWire News)*
- **First randomization (R1, week −8):** 714 patients assigned to empagliflozin 10 mg daily or matching placebo for an 8-week run-in. *(Source: DocWire News summary of AJKD paper)*
- **Second randomization (R2, week 0):** 586 patients assigned to vicadrostat 3 mg, 10 mg, or 20 mg daily, or vicadrostat-matched placebo, added to their R1 background. *(Source: DocWire News; consistent with europepmc records citing n=586)*
- **Primary outcome:** change in urine albumin-to-creatinine ratio (UACR) from R2 baseline to week 14. This post hoc analysis estimated the **cumulative** effect of vicadrostat + EMPA 10 mg on UACR versus vicadrostat alone. *(Source: metabolismjournal.com abstract listing)*

## Baseline characteristics (R1 set, n=714)

- Mean age 63.9 years; 60.2% White; 69.5% with T2D. *(Source: DocWire News summary of AJKD paper)*
- Mean eGFR 53.3 mL/min/1.73 m²; median UACR 691.5 mg/g at R1 baseline. *(Source: DocWire News summary of AJKD paper)*

## Key results (verified findings)

- **Largest UACR reduction at week 14 with combination therapy (VicaEmpa):** ~**61%** with vicadrostat 10 mg + empagliflozin, versus **40%** with vicadrostat 10 mg alone, **26%** with empagliflozin alone, and **3%** with dual-matched placebo. *(Source: DocWire News; ScienceDirect AJKD snippet — "largest reduction in UACRFMV of 61% … versus 40% with vicadrostat (10 mg) and 26% with [empagliflozin]")*
- Mean UACR reductions **exceeded 30% across all VicaEmpa dose groups**, described as clinically meaningful for long-term kidney outcomes. *(Source: DocWire News summary of AJKD paper)*
- VicaEmpa also produced larger acute decreases in eGFR (from R1 to week 14) and larger reductions in systolic blood pressure than the comparators. *(Source: DocWire News summary of AJKD paper)*

## Safety

- Interventions to lower potassium and treatment discontinuations were infrequent; no unexpected safety signals were observed with vicadrostat or VicaEmpa. *(Source: DocWire News summary of AJKD paper)*

## Context (from a related rationale paper, for background only)

- Prior phase 2 data from the same 586-patient albuminuric CKD population showed 10 mg vicadrostat reduced UACR by ~40% vs placebo, with or without empagliflozin; the pathway is being advanced in the phase 3 EASi-KIDNEY trial (NCT06531824, ~11,000 patients). *(Source: Judge PK et al., Nephrol Dial Transplant 2025, doi:10.1093/ndt/gfae263, PMID 39533115, europepmc abstract)*
